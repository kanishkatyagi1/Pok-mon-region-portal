Pokémon Center

A Pokémon Center is a type of building that provides regulatory services for Pokémon Trainers.Our Pokémon Centers are to accommodate Trainers in need and they serve as a resting spot and healing of your pokemons completely free of charge.Our Pokémon Center can be find near forest or caves.They can be found by their red roof, and the giant 'P'.
In our Pokémon Centers, the Wireless Cable Club and the lobby hold the PC. Our Center contains professional Nurses. Our Nurse will heal all of your Pokémon, returning their HP and PP to normal and healing their status aliments. Located on the far end of the Center are the PC and the Link . The Link Club allows you to link with other trainers and trade and battle others. Whereas the PC allows access to your stored Pokémon and even to have your Pokédex rated by the known Professor, Professor Ock.
